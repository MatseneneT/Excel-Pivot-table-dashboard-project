# Excel-Pivot-table-dashboard-project
I developed a comprehensive dashboard in Excel to analyze data. This process included data cleaning, data visualization by transforming raw customer data into an interactive bike sales dashboard in Excel using Pivot tables to assist with Business decision making.
## Data set used
-<a href="https://github.com/AlexTheAnalyst/Excel-Tutorial/blob/main/Excel%20Project%20Dataset.xlsx">Data Set</a>

## Objectives:
-How does average income affect a customers decision to buy a bike?
- Does commute distance influence bike purchases?
- Which age group is most likely to buy a bike?
Dashboard Image: 
## Key insights:
- Average income affects positively a customers decision to buy a bike. Customer's with higher income are more likely to buy a bike
- The highest number of purchases comes from customers with a 5-10 minute commute, indicating the bikes are often used for practical travel
- The middle aged group (31-54 years old) are most likely to buy a bike. representing the company largest and most valuable data base
